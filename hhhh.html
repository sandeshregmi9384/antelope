About Us

Welcome to The Antelope, your friendly village pub in the heart of Lighthorne. We offer hearty traditional meals, perfectly poured pints, and a warm, community-focused atmosphere. Whether you're joining us for Sunday roast or a midweek quiz, you're always among friends here.

Our Menu

Sunday Roast

Succulent roast meats, golden potatoes, seasonal vegetables, Yorkshire pudding, and rich gravy.

Fish & Chips

Crispy battered fish served with chunky chips, mushy peas, and tartar sauce.

Steak & Ale Pie

Homemade pie filled with tender steak and rich ale gravy, served with creamy mashed potatoes.

Events & Specials

Join us for weekly quiz nights every Wednesday, live music every Friday, and seasonal specials throughout the year. Follow us on social media for updates!
